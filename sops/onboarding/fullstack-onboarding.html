Your Onboarding Journey

Follow these 4 steps to become a certified Fullstack Developer.

1

📖 Master the SOP

Your first task is to read, understand, and internalize the Standard Operating Procedure. This document provides the engineering standards and workflows.

Go to Fullstack SOP
2

🎥 Watch Training Videos

Visit the Assets Library and watch the required training videos. These will give you visual context to the written SOPs.

Open Assets Library
3

🎓 Pass the Knowledge Test

Prove your understanding by taking the role-specific quiz. You need a score of 90% or higher to proceed.

ℹ️ How to take the test: Go back to the Fullstack SOP and click the "🎓 Test" button in the sidebar.

4

🎙️ The 30-Minute Recording Task

This is your final verification. Record a 30-minute screen sharing video walking us through your understanding of the role.

What to cover in your video:
  • Walk through the SOP sections and explain them in your own words.
  • Explain your KRA (Key Result Areas) and how you plan to achieve them.
  • Explain the KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) you are measured on.
  • Demonstrate how you would handle a mock development task.
