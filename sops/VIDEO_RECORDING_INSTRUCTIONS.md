## üìã General Video Best Practices (Apply to ALL SOPs)

### ‚úÖ DO's
- **Be conversational** ‚Äî Talk like you're explaining to a friend, not reading a script
- **Use your own words** ‚Äî 80% should be YOUR understanding of the principles
- **Show real examples** ‚Äî Open actual stores, tools, or screens to demonstrate
- **Add 1-2 line personal insights** ‚Äî "From my experience..." or "What I've learned is..."
- **Use simple language** ‚Äî Explain like teaching a 5-year-old who knows nothing
- **Pause and emphasize** ‚Äî Slow down on critical points
- **Give context** ‚Äî Explain WHY things matter, not just WHAT to do

### ‚ùå DON'Ts
- Don't read the SOP word-for-word (robotic)
- Don't rush through sections
- Don't assume prior knowledge
- Don't skip examples
- Don't forget to show the actual tools/screens

### üé• Video Format
- **Length:** 15-30 minutes per SOP (split into parts if longer)
- **Tool:** Loom (screen + face recording)
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum
- **Audio:** Clear, no background noise
- **Screen:** Show SOP document + live demos side by side
