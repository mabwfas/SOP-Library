Your Daily Routine

⏰ Work Hours

Standard work hours: 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM IST (flexible by role)
You must be online and responsive during your work hours.

↩️ ⚠️ CRITICAL: Response Time SLA

Failure to respond during work hours = Marked ABSENT for the day

Message From Response Time
Management/CEO IMMEDIATE (5 minutes)
Team Lead Within 10 minutes
Colleagues Within 30 minutes
Clients (Fiverr) Within 1 hour

✅ All assigned tasks must be acknowledged in the same message thread

📱 Discord Notification = MANDATORY

  • Turn ON Discord notifications on ALL devices during work hours
  • Install Discord on your PC/laptop for instant access
  • If communicated via phone call, still respond in Discord group for documentation
  • Every message mentioning you requires a response — no exceptions

Acceptable acknowledgments: "Okay", "Yes sir/ma'am", "Working on it", "Understood", "Will do"

📍 Daily Start Time Posting

⚠️ CRITICAL: Post BEFORE 11:00 AM every day - no exceptions

  • Format: "I'll start working from [TIME]" or "I started working at [TIME]"
  • If starting late (after 12 PM), post by 10:55 AM with expected start time
  • Team Lead posts consolidated list of all working members by 11:30 AM

🏖️ Leave Request Protocol

  • Leave request must be posted BEFORE 12:00 PM (noon) on leave day
  • Format: "Leave - [DATE] - Reason: [SPECIFIC REASON]"
  • ⚠️ Half days DO NOT exist (except Saturday) - mark full leave
  • If on leave, still check group once for urgent messages

💬 Overcommunication Principle

Fast communication is a form of RESPECT.

  • Never ignore anyone's messages — clients, teammates, superiors, or juniors
  • Provide updates even when not asked
  • If unable to complete task, respond with reason + alternative timeline

Morning Startup (First 15 Minutes)

🌅 Start of Day Checklist

  • Set your Discord status to 🟢 Online
  • Check Discord for any overnight messages
  • 🔴 CONNECT WITH YOUR MANAGER — Brief check-in call or message to get daily priorities
  • Review your tasks in project management tool (Notion/Trello/ClickUp)
  • Check email for client or team updates
  • Read any SOPs relevant to today's work
  • Plan your top 3 priorities for the day

During Work Hours

Time Activity Notes
10:00 AM Go Online + Morning Check-in Set Discord status, review priorities
10:15 AM - 1:00 PM Deep Work Block 1 Focus on highest-priority tasks
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Lunch Break Set status to 🍽️ Lunch
2:00 PM - 4:00 PM Deep Work Block 2 Continue priority tasks
4:00 PM - 5:00 PM Meetings / Collaboration Client calls, team syncs
5:00 PM - 6:30 PM Deep Work Block 3 Finish remaining tasks
6:30 PM - 7:00 PM Wrap Up + EOD Update Update tasks, send EOD report

End of Day (Last 15 Minutes)

🌙 End of Day Checklist

  • Update all task statuses in project tool
  • Send EOD update to your manager/channel
  • Reply to any pending Discord messages
  • Note any blockers for tomorrow
  • Set Discord status to 🔴 Offline

📋 EOD Report - MANDATORY by 9:30 PM

⚠️ Be SPECIFIC — include exact numbers, not vague descriptions

REQUIRED FORMAT:

[Full Name]

Today I worked on:

1. [Client Name] - [Specific task] - [Number of items]

2. [Second task if applicable]


Store Link: [Full URL]

Password: [Store password]

Completion: [X]% | Blockers: [List or 'None']

Specificity Requirements:

  • Include exact number of products added/edited
  • Include exact number of pages customized
  • Include percentage completion of assigned project
  • Include ETA for remaining work
  • Update task tracker spreadsheet same day

💡 Talk in NUMBERS most of the time — dates, quantities, percentages

Communication Rules

💬 Discord is Our HQ

Discord is our primary communication tool. Being active on Discord = being at work.

⏱️
1-Hour Response Rule
During work hours, reply to ALL messages within 1 hour. No exceptions.
🟢
Online Status Required
Keep your status set to Online during work hours. Change it when on break or unavailable.
📱
Mobile App Installed
Install Discord on your phone for urgent messages when away from desk.
🔔
Notifications ON
Enable notifications for mentions and DMs. Mute only non-essential channels.

🚨 What Happens If You Don't Respond?

  • First occurrence: Friendly reminder from manager
  • Repeated: Logged as communication issue
  • Chronic: Performance review impact

If you need to step away, update your status and inform your manager. Communication is non-negotiable.

↩️ Response Priority Guide

Message Type Response Time Priority
Client emergency / Site down Immediately (within 15 mins) URGENT
Manager / Team Lead DM Within 30 minutes HIGH
Team channel mentions Within 1 hour MEDIUM
General channel messages Within 2 hours NORMAL

✅ Communication Best Practices

  • Acknowledge messages with a reaction if you need time to respond
  • Use threads to keep conversations organized
  • Tag the right people — don't spam @everyone unless necessary
  • Keep messages clear and concise — get to the point
  • Use voice channels for complex discussions

Time Management

⏰ The 80/20 Rule

80% of your results come from 20% of your tasks. Identify your high-impact work and protect time for it.

Productivity Principles

🎯 Do This

  • ✅ Start with your hardest task
  • ✅ Time-block deep work sessions
  • ✅ Take regular short breaks
  • ✅ Set daily goals (max 3 priorities)
  • ✅ Track your time on tasks

🚫 Avoid This

  • ❌ Checking email/Discord constantly
  • ❌ Multitasking on important work
  • ❌ Skipping breaks (leads to burnout)
  • ❌ Saying yes to everything
  • ❌ Working without a plan

If You're Falling Behind

  1. Don't panic — assess what's actually urgent
  2. Communicate early — tell your manager BEFORE deadlines are missed
  3. Ask for help — someone might be able to assist
  4. Prioritize ruthlessly — what can wait vs. what can't?
  5. Learn from it — why did this happen? How to prevent it?

Daily Reporting

📊 End of Day (EOD) Update

Every day before logging off, send a brief update to your manager or designated channel.

EOD Update Format

📅 EOD Update - [Your Name] - [Date]

✅ Completed Today:
• [Task 1]
• [Task 2]
• [Task 3]

🔄 In Progress:
• [Task with % complete]

Tomorrow's Priority:
• [Main focus for tomorrow]

🚧 Blockers:
• [Any blockers or issues] (or "None")

⚠️ EOD Updates Are Mandatory

Missing EOD updates will be tracked. If you need to log off early, send your EOD before leaving. No update = assumed you didn't work.
