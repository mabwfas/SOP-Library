Time Management

‚è∞ The 80/20 Rule

80% of your results come from 20% of your tasks. Identify your high-impact work and protect time for it.

Productivity Principles

üéØ Do This

  • ‚úÖ Start with your hardest task
  • ‚úÖ Time-block deep work sessions
  • ‚úÖ Take regular short breaks
  • ‚úÖ Set daily goals (max 3 priorities)
  • ‚úÖ Track your time on tasks

üö´ Avoid This

  • ‚ùå Checking email/Discord constantly
  • ‚ùå Multitasking on important work
  • ‚ùå Skipping breaks (leads to burnout)
  • ‚ùå Saying yes to everything
  • ‚ùå Working without a plan

If You're Falling Behind

  1. Don't panic ‚Äî assess what's actually urgent
  2. Communicate early ‚Äî tell your manager BEFORE deadlines are missed
  3. Ask for help ‚Äî someone might be able to assist
  4. Prioritize ruthlessly ‚Äî what can wait vs. what can't?
  5. Learn from it ‚Äî why did this happen? How to prevent it?
