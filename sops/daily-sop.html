🚨 What Happens If You Don't Respond?

  • First occurrence: Friendly reminder from manager
  • Repeated: Logged as communication issue
  • Chronic: Performance review impact

If you need to step away, update your status and inform your manager. Communication is non-negotiable.

↩️ Response Priority Guide

Message Type Response Time Priority
Client emergency / Site down Immediately (within 15 mins) URGENT
Manager / Team Lead DM Within 30 minutes HIGH
Team channel mentions Within 1 hour MEDIUM
General channel messages Within 2 hours NORMAL

✅ Communication Best Practices

  • Acknowledge messages with a reaction if you need time to respond
  • Use threads to keep conversations organized
  • Tag the right people — don't spam @everyone unless necessary
  • Keep messages clear and concise — get to the point
  • Use voice channels for complex discussions

Time Management

⏰ The 80/20 Rule

80% of your results come from 20% of your tasks. Identify your high-impact work and protect time for it.

Productivity Principles

🎯 Do This

  • ✅ Start with your hardest task
  • ✅ Time-block deep work sessions
  • ✅ Take regular short breaks
  • ✅ Set daily goals (max 3 priorities)
  • ✅ Track your time on tasks

🚫 Avoid This

  • ❌ Checking email/Discord constantly
  • ❌ Multitasking on important work
  • ❌ Skipping breaks (leads to burnout)
  • ❌ Saying yes to everything
  • ❌ Working without a plan

If You're Falling Behind

  1. Don't panic — assess what's actually urgent
  2. Communicate early — tell your manager BEFORE deadlines are missed
  3. Ask for help — someone might be able to assist
  4. Prioritize ruthlessly — what can wait vs. what can't?
  5. Learn from it — why did this happen? How to prevent it?

Daily Reporting

📊 End of Day (EOD) Update

Every day before logging off, send a brief update to your manager or designated channel.

EOD Update Format

📅 EOD Update - [Your Name] - [Date]

✅ Completed Today:
• [Task 1]
• [Task 2]
• [Task 3]

🔄 In Progress:
• [Task with % complete]

Tomorrow's Priority:
• [Main focus for tomorrow]

🚧 Blockers:
• [Any blockers or issues] (or "None")

⚠️ EOD Updates Are Mandatory

Missing EOD updates will be tracked. If you need to log off early, send your EOD before leaving. No update = assumed you didn't work.
