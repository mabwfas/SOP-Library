Frequency Asked Questions (FAQ)

Everything you need to know about surviving and thriving at Digital Heroes.

üí∞ Salary & Payments

1. When exactly will I get my salary?
Your salary is credited before the 10th of the following month. For example, your January salary will be credited by February 10th.
2. What is the salary cycle?
We calculate salaries from the 1st to the End of the Month. Attendance and performance during this period determine your payout.
3. Can I discuss my salary with my colleague?
ABSOLUTELY NOT. Salary confidentiality is a zero-tolerance policy. Discussing it leads to immediate termination for both parties involved.
4. How do I get a raise?
Deliver more value. Increases are based on Performance (KPIs), not seniority. If you hit "Exceptional" (90%+ KPI), you can get a 15-20% hike.
5. Do we get Diwali bonuses?
Yes, festival bonuses are distributed based on company performance and your tenure/contribution. It is not a fixed entitlement but a celebration of shared success.
6. I need money urgently. Can I get an advance?
Yes, if you have completed 3 months. You can request up to 50% of your salary for genuine emergencies (medical/family). It will be deducted over the next 2 months.
7. Will I get paid for overtime?
No. We value output, not hours. If you need extra time to deliver excellence, that helps you achieve your KPIs and quarterly bonuses. We don't pay hourly; we pay for results.
8. Is my salary taxable?
TDS is deducted as per government regulations if your salary exceeds the taxable bracket. You will receive Form 16 annually.
9. What if there is a mistake in my salary?
Contact HR (Agrima) immediately via Discord/Email. We will rectify legitimate errors within 48 hours.

‚è∞ Attendance & Leaves

10. What happens if I am 5 minutes late?
0-15 mins grace period is allowed occasionally. However, arriving after 9:15 AM triggers a Half-Day Salary Deduction.
11. Can I take leave during my probation?
Generally, NO. Leaves during the first 2 months are not allowed unless it is a medical emergency. Focus on learning.
12. How many leaves do I get per year?
12 Casual Leaves (CL), 6 Sick Leaves (SL), and ~10 National Holidays. Earned Leaves (EL) start accruing after 1 year.
13. Can I carry forward my CLs to next year?
No. Casual Leaves and Sick Leaves lapse at the end of the year. Use them for your well-being.
14. What if I fall sick suddenly?
Inform your manager by 9:00 AM via WhatsApp/Discord. If you are out for more than 2 consecutive days, you MUST submit a medical certificate.
15. Can I take a planned holiday?
Yes, but you must apply at least 3 days in advance. Approval depends on work coverage.
16. Can I take leave between 25th and 5th of the month?
Strictly NO. This is our billing and reporting cycle. Leaves are blocked during this period unless it is a severe emergency.
17. What is "Unexplained Absence"?
If you don't show up and don't message by 9:00 AM, it is Unexplained.
1st time: Warning.
3rd time: 3 Days Pay Cut.
5th time: Termination.

üè† Work From Home (WFH)

18. Am I eligible for WFH?
Only after completing 3 months (Probation) AND maintaining good performance. It is a privilege, not a right.
19. How many WFH days can I take?
Maximum 2 days per week. They cannot be consecutive (e.g., no Monday + Tuesday).
20. Can I work from a cafe?
Only if the internet is stable (10+ Mbps) and it's quiet enough for calls. Your environment must not hinder your productivity.
21. What if my internet goes down during WFH?
You are expected to have a backup (mobile hotspot). If you cannot work, you must take a leave (Half day/Full day).
22. Can I refuse a WFH request from my manager?
We rarely force WFH, but if the office is closed (e.g., maintenance), you are expected to work remotely.

üö® Red Lines (Zero Tolerance)

23. I didn't see the client's message. Will I be fired?
If you ignore direct messages from management/team repeatedly, YES. One missed message is a mistake; ignoring is a habit. We fire for the habit.
24. Can I do freelance work on weekends?
NO. While you are employed here, you are exclusive to Digital Heroes. Moonlighting is grounds for immediate termination.
25. Can I use my personal laptop for work?
Only with explicit permission. Client data must NEVER be stored on personal unsecured devices.
26. What counts as a "Data Breach"?
Sharing passwords, sharing client lists, downloading code to personal drives, or showing internal docs to outsiders. This leads to Legal Action.
27. I made a mistake. Should I hide it?
NEVER. Hiding a mistake is worse than the mistake itself. Own it, report it, fix it. We forgive errors; we do not forgive cover-ups.
