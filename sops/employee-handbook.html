Frequently Asked Questions

Attendance & Timing

Q: What happens if I am late (after 9:15)?
Half-day salary deduction. No exceptions. If you have a specific reason, inform BEFORE 9 AM, but deduction usually still applies.
Q: Can I leave early if work is done?
No. Office hours are 9-6. If free, upskill or help teammates. Early leave requires approval.

Leaves

Q: Can I take more than 2 CL in a month?
Requires Senior Management approval + 3 days advance notice.
Q: Can I take leave between 25th-5th?
Only for medical emergencies or own wedding. This is blackout period for billing.

Work From Home

Q: Can manager reject WFH without reason?
Yes. WFH is a privilege. If abused or performance is low, it is denied.
Q: What if my internet fails on WFH?
Go to office. Unstable net is not an excuse for missed work.

Salary

Q: Can I discuss salary with colleagues?
Allowing salary discussion is grounds for immediate termination (NDA Violation).
Q: How do I get an increment?
Quarterly/Annual review based on KPI. You must earn it through performance (90%+ score).

General

Q: Can I freelance on the side?
No. Moonlighting without written approval = Termination + Legal Action.
Q: Who do I contact for complaints?
HR (Agrima) first. Then Ops Head (Anurag) if unresolved.
