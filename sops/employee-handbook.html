Work From Home (WFH)

‚úÖ Eligibility

Only after probation (3 months) AND with manager approval. WFH is a privilege, not a right.

Rules

  • Max Days: 2 days per week (Must be non-consecutive).
  • Response Time: Must respond within 30 mins during work hours.
  • Status: Post "Online - WFH" on Discord by 9:15 AM.
  • Camera: Must be ON for all meetings.

Revocation: If you are unresponsive or unproductive on WFH days, this privilege will be permanently revoked.

Policies & Code of Conduct

üö´ ZERO TOLERANCE: Negativity & Politics

We have an absolute Zero Tolerance Policy for office politics, gossip, or spreading negativity.

  • Do not complain to colleagues about management/work. Raise issues directly to HR/Management.
  • Do not form "cliques" or groups that exclude others.
  • Do not bad-mouth clients or teammates.

Creating a toxic environment is the fastest way to get terminated, regardless of your performance.

üîí Confidentiality (NDA)

You must not share client data, code, strategies, or salary info with anyone. Breach = Immediate Termination + Legal Action.

Anti-Harassment

We are a safe workplace. Any harassment (verbal, sexual, bullying) will result in immediate investigation and potential termination. Report directly to HR or Delhi Team.

Clear, Direct, Professional. Read messages completely. Respond within 2 hours.
