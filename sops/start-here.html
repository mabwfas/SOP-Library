Start Here

Welcome to Digital Heroes! Follow these 5 steps to go from new joiner to full team member.

🎬 Step 1

Watch, Read & Explore

Get to know Digital Heroes — who we are, how we work, and where everything lives. These two videos walk you through the company, the Employee Handbook, and how to navigate the SOP Library.

🎥 Welcome to Digital Heroes
🎥 Navigating the SOP Library
  • ✓ Watch both welcome videos above to understand our company culture and values
  • ✓ Read the Employee Handbook — covers work culture, leave policies, WFH rules, and 60+ FAQs
  • ✓ Explore the SOP Library — find your role's SOP, KRA, KPI, and DTS pages
  • ✓ Browse the Onboarding Hub — role-specific Day 1 guides and tool setup
  • ✓ Go through AI Training — learn Claude Code, MCP servers, and AI tools we use every day
💡 Tip: Don't rush this step. Spend a full day reading through your role's SOP and the handbook. The better you understand how DH works, the faster you'll start contributing.
🎓 Step 2

Complete Your Role Training & Get Certified

Every role at DH has a dedicated training program with lessons, quizzes, and a certificate. You must score 100% on every quiz to unlock your certificate — this proves you've mastered the fundamentals.

  • ✓ Go to the Training Hub and find your role's training module
  • ✓ Complete all tasks and pass every quiz with a perfect score
  • ✓ Download your certificate once all tasks are complete
  • ✓ Share your certificate with us — email it to hr@digitalheroes.co.in
🎓 Open Training Hub
ℹ️ Note: If you fail a quiz, review the material and retry. There's no limit on attempts — take your time and learn the content properly.
📝 Step 3

Complete Your Practical Assignment

Now it's time to prove your skills with a hands-on task. Every role has a practical assignment that simulates real DH work — complete it and submit through the form.

  • ✓ Check with your reporting manager or HR for your role-specific practical assignment
  • ✓ Complete the assignment within the given deadline (usually 24–72 hours)
  • ✓ Submit your work through the submission form provided with the assignment
  • ✓ Your submission will be reviewed and scored — top performers get priority for higher stipend brackets
💡 Tip: Treat this like a real client project. Attention to detail, meeting the deadline, and going the extra mile will set you apart from other candidates.
📄 Step 4

Submit Your Documents & Stipend Details

Almost there! Fill out the stipend form, attach your training certificate, and submit all required joining documents to HR.

  • ✓ Fill out the stipend details form (bank account, PAN, Aadhaar details)
  • ✓ Attach your training certificate (downloaded in Step 2)
  • ✓ Email everything to hr@digitalheroes.co.in with subject: "Joining Docs — [Your Name] — [Role]"
  • ✓ HR will confirm receipt and share your official start date and team assignments
📋 Documents Checklist: Resume, Aadhaar Card (front & back), PAN Card, Bank passbook/cancelled cheque, Passport-size photo, Training certificate, Signed offer letter.
🤝 Step 5

Join the Mission — You're One of Us Now

You're not just an employee — you're a Digital Hero. We're a team of builders, creators, and problem-solvers working toward one goal: helping businesses grow through world-class digital experiences.

  • 🔥 Join your team's daily standup — share what you're working on, ask questions, and stay aligned with the team
  • 💬 Introduce yourself on the team group — tell everyone who you are, your role, and one fun fact about yourself
  • 🎯 Set your first week goals with your reporting manager — know exactly what success looks like in Week 1
  • 🤝 Find your buddy — every new joiner gets paired with an experienced team member who'll help you get up to speed
  • 🚀 Start shipping — pick up your first task, deliver quality work, and earn your stripes. At DH, we respect people who execute.
🦸
