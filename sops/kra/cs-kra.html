📝 Role Overview

Client Success is responsible for post-delivery client satisfaction, ensuring every client leaves a 5-star review and becomes a repeat customer. This role owns the handover experience and ongoing relationship.

🚀 Primary Mission: Turn every delivery into a 5-star review and upsell opportunity.
