Key Result Areas – What You're Responsible For

Compelling Copy 💰 Conversion Focus 🔎 🔎 SEO Optimized

📝 Role Overview

The Content Writer is responsible for creating compelling, conversion-focused copy for all client projects. This role owns product descriptions, website copy, and all written content.

🚀 Primary Mission: Write copy that converts visitors into customers.

🎯 Key Result Areas

1. Product Descriptions

  • Benefit-focused copy
  • Feature-to-benefit conversion
  • Scannable formatting
  • SEO keywords

2. Website Copy

  • Homepage messaging
  • About page storytelling
  • Collection descriptions
  • Policy pages

💰 3. Conversion Copy

  • CTAs & buttons
  • Trust-building content
  • Urgency messaging
  • Social proof

🔎 🔎 4. SEO Writing

  • Keyword integration
  • Meta descriptions
  • Alt text
  • Heading structure

5. AI Tool Usage

  • ChatGPT prompting
  • Content enhancement
  • Editing & refinement
  • Quality control

✅ 6. Quality Assurance

  • Grammar & spelling
  • Brand voice consistency
  • Fact checking
  • Revision turnaround
