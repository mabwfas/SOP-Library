Morning Block (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

  • Check all client channels - Fiverr, Upwork, Email, Slack
  • Respond to queries - Within 1 hour of receiving
  • Review delivered projects - Check client satisfaction status
  • Follow up on pending reviews - Request 5-star reviews
  • Check support tickets - Address post-delivery issues

Afternoon Block (12:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

  • Client check-in calls - Scheduled relationship calls
  • Upsell opportunities - Identify and propose add-ons
  • Feedback collection - Gather testimonials and case studies
  • Training resources - Create guides for clients
  • CRM updates - Log all interactions

Evening Block (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

  • Final message sweep - No message left unread
  • Review request follow-ups - Gentle reminders for reviews
  • EOD status report - Client satisfaction metrics
  • Plan tomorrow's outreach - Schedule proactive check-ins

Pro Tip

The project doesn't end at delivery. That's where the REAL relationship begins. Happy clients become repeat clients and referral sources.
