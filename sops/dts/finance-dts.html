Morning Block (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

  • Check bank accounts - Verify incoming payments
  • Reconcile Fiverr/Upwork - Match platform earnings to bank
  • Process invoices - Create and send pending invoices
  • Expense approvals - Review and approve expense claims
  • Cash flow check - Monitor current cash position

Afternoon Block (12:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

  • Payment processing - Vendor and contractor payments
  • GST/Tax updates - Log transactions for tax compliance
  • Payroll prep - Update salary data as needed
  • Budget tracking - Compare actuals vs budget
  • Vendor follow-ups - Chase pending invoices

Evening Block (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

  • Daily entries - Complete all accounting entries
  • Report updates - Update financial dashboards
  • EOD status - Report any cash flow concerns
  • Plan tomorrow - Identify pending financial tasks

Pro Tip

Reconcile daily, not monthly. Small discrepancies caught early are easy to fix. Month-end surprises are nightmares.
