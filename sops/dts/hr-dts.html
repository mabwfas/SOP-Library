Morning Block (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

  • Attendance check - Verify team check-ins and absences
  • Review job applications - Screen new CVs for open positions
  • Schedule interviews - Coordinate with candidates and interviewers
  • Onboarding tasks - Support new joiners
  • Leave approvals - Process pending leave requests

Afternoon Block (12:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

  • Conduct interviews - Phone screens and interviews
  • Employee queries - Address HR-related questions
  • Documentation - Update employee records
  • Performance tracking - Follow up on PIPs and reviews
  • Policy compliance - Ensure policy adherence

Evening Block (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

  • Candidate follow-ups - Send feedback and next steps
  • Payroll updates - Log any changes for payroll
  • EOD status report - Hiring and HR metrics
  • Plan tomorrow - Schedule interviews and tasks

Pro Tip

Hire slow, fire fast. Take your time finding the right people but don't hesitate to act quickly when someone isn't working out.
