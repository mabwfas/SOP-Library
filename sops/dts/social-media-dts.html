Morning Block (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

  • Check all platforms - IG, LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter
  • Respond to comments/DMs - Engage with audience
  • Check scheduled posts - Verify today's content is ready
  • Monitor mentions - Brand mentions and tags
  • Trend research - Check trending topics/hashtags

Afternoon Block (12:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

  • Content creation - Create posts for upcoming days
  • Write captions - Engaging copy with CTAs
  • Design graphics - Create visual content
  • Schedule content - Queue posts for optimal times
  • Community engagement - Comment on relevant accounts

Evening Block (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

  • Post engagement check - Respond to new interactions
  • Analytics review - Check daily performance
  • EOD status report - Social media metrics
  • Content ideas - Brainstorm for upcoming content

Pro Tip

Consistency beats virality. Regular, quality content builds more lasting engagement than chasing viral moments.
