Content Writer Daily Task Sheet

Your daily checklist for content excellence

Morning Block (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

  • Check content requests - Review new assignments from PM
  • Research client brand - Understand brand voice and audience
  • Outline content - Plan structure before writing
  • Product descriptions - Write compelling product copy
  • SEO keyword research - Identify target keywords

Afternoon Block (12:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

  • Content writing - Deep focus on assigned content
  • Blog posts - Write SEO-optimized articles
  • Email copy - Welcome sequences, promotions
  • Website copy - Homepage, About, Collection pages
  • Self-edit - Proofread before submission

Evening Block (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

  • Submit completed content - Send for PM review
  • Address revisions - Implement feedback quickly
  • EOD status report - Content metrics and progress
  • Read and learn - Study great copywriting examples

Pro Tip

Features tell, benefits sell. Don't just describe what a product is - explain what it does for the customer and how it makes them feel.
