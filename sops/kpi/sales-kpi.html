💰 Sales Executive KPI

Key Performance Indicators – How We Measure Success

📈 Revenue Metrics 💰 Conversion Rates ↩️ 📅 Response Time

📝 KPI Overview

These KPIs measure the effectiveness of the Sales Executive role. Performance is tracked weekly and reviewed monthly.

🎯 Key Performance Indicators

Monthly Revenue

$15,000+
Total revenue from closed deals per month
Sum of all closed order values

💰 Conversion Rate

25%+
Percentage of leads converted to paying clients
(Closed Deals / Total Leads) × 100

↩️ Response Time

< 30 min
Average time to first response on new leads
Avg(First Response - Lead Received)

Quote-to-Close

40%+
Percentage of quotes that become orders
(Closed Orders / Quotes Sent) × 100

Average Order Value

$800+
Average value per closed deal
Total Revenue / Number of Orders

Daily Activities

20+ touches
Calls, emails, and follow-ups per day
Sum of all client interactions
